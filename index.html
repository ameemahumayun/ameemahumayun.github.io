About

About

I work at the intersection of UX research, human-centered AI, and culturally situated design — mostly in healthcare and privacy, where standard product assumptions break down. My research is published at CHI and SOUPS (both CORE A*), with a third manuscript in submission to Nature Health. The work spans fieldwork, multi-turn evaluation of frontier models, and the translation of qualitative findings into concrete design and product implications.

I'm drawn to ambiguous problems and sensitive contexts — the places where understanding what people actually need is harder than building the interface. I work in Urdu, English, and Punjabi, which directly shapes my research on multilingual and culturally grounded AI.
